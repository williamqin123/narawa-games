Troubleshooting

These videos are from YouTube. School districts may block YouTube. If you are trying to watch these videos on a public school network, it might not work. If you really want to watch the video, ask a teacher or staff member to un-block Narawa Games and YouTube.

If a video shows up but doesn't play, there might be some technical issues. If the address at the top of your browser says “http://”, then change it to say “https://”, and see if that works.

If you can watch a video, but they appear pixelated or “jerky”, you probably have a slow internet connection. Change the quality of the video to 480P or 720P, and see if the quality of the video gets better.
